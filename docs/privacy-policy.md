# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-30

Style AI ("we", "our", "us") provides a mobile app for wardrobe management and style recommendations.

## Data We Collect
- Account data (email address for authentication)
- User-provided wardrobe content (photos, item metadata)
- Optional location data (for weather-based recommendations)
- Basic app diagnostics needed to operate and improve the app

## How We Use Data
- Authenticate and secure user accounts
- Save and sync wardrobe data
- Generate personalized style suggestions
- Provide weather-aware recommendations when location permission is granted

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell personal data.
- We use Supabase as our backend provider to process and store app data.
- Data may be disclosed when required by law.

## Data Retention
- Data is retained while your account is active, or as needed to provide services.
- You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support.

## Permissions
- Camera and photo library permissions are used to add wardrobe photos.
- Location permission is optional and used only for weather-related features.

## Security
- We use industry-standard controls and managed infrastructure providers.
- No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

## Contact
For privacy requests, contact: support@styleai.app
